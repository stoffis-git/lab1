Frequently Asked Questions

How long until I get results?

Results are typically available within 5-7 business days after your sample arrives at our laboratory.

Is the test safe?

Yes, completely safe. A simple finger-prick blood sample is all that's needed. Detailed instructions are included.

Can I take multiple tests?

Absolutely! Many customers start with one test and add others later. We offer bundle discounts for multiple tests.

What happens after I get results?

Your personalized supplementation journey begins! You'll receive custom formulations based on your unique blood+DNA insights, access our mobile app to track progress, and get ongoing support.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Core Line testing

How does the testing process work? ▼

After ordering, you'll receive a test kit at home containing everything needed for DNA (cheek swab) and blood collection. You can either collect samples at home and mail them back, or visit a partner collection site. Results are typically ready in 2-3 weeks.

What's included in my monthly subscription? ▼

Your subscription includes your personalized daily supplement packs, full access to the Lab1 app with biomarker tracking, and one consultation with our nutrition team. Retesting is recommended every 6 months (additional cost).

Can I switch between Nutrition, Performance, and Longevity tests? ▼

Yes! Your DNA data remains valid, so switching focus areas only requires new blood work to analyze the relevant biomarkers. Contact our support team to coordinate the switch.

Is my genetic and health data private? ▼

Absolutely. Your data is encrypted end-to-end, stored on EU servers complying with GDPR, and never shared with third parties without your explicit consent. You own your data and can request deletion at any time.

Can I cancel anytime? ▼

Yes, you can cancel your supplement subscription anytime with no penalties. Your test results and data remain accessible in your account even after cancellation.

Do you ship internationally? ▼

Currently, we ship within the EU and UK. Testing services are available in Germany, Austria, Switzerland, Netherlands, and Belgium. Contact us for updates on expansion to other regions.
