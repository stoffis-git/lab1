Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about Essential Line

How do I choose which Essential formula is right for me? ▼

Each formula is designed for specific demographics based on population research. Choose based on your age, gender, diet (omnivore vs plant-based), and lifestyle (sedentary vs active). If you're unsure, take our product quiz or contact our support team.

What makes Essential Line different from drugstore supplements? ▼

We use exclusively bioavailable forms (like methylfolate instead of folic acid, magnesium glycinate instead of oxide) at therapeutic doses proven in research. Most drugstore brands use cheap, poorly absorbed forms at sub-optimal doses.

Can I see the full ingredient list and dosages? ▼

Absolutely! Click on any individual formula (E1-E6) to see the complete ingredient breakdown with exact dosages, forms used, and the research behind each nutrient.

How long until I notice results? ▼

Most customers report improved energy within 2-4 weeks. Full benefits typically manifest after 2-3 months of consistent use as nutrient stores replenish. Some nutrients (like vitamin D, iron, B12) may take longer if you're starting from deficiency.

Can I upgrade to Core Line later? ▼

Yes! Essential customers get €50 off Core Line testing. Simply contact support to upgrade. Your supplement subscription will pause during testing, then resume with your personalized formula.
