Frequently Asked Questions

Can I take this if I have MTHFR variants?

Yes! The methylfolate (5-MTHF) form works for everyone, including those with MTHFR variants. However, if you have the TT variant (more severe), you may need higher doses found in Core Line.

Will this cause constipation like other iron supplements?

No. We use iron bisglycinate specifically because it's gentle on the stomach and doesn't cause constipation like ferrous sulfate.

How is this different from a regular multivitamin?

Premium bioavailable forms (10x cost but actually absorbed), therapeutic doses (not token amounts), and targeted to your demographic's specific needs based on population research.

When should I take this?

Take with food for best absorption. Iron is best absorbed with vitamin C (included), away from calcium-rich foods. Can split into AM/PM doses if preferred.
