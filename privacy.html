Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

At Lab1 (operated by AMO GmbH), we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information, including sensitive health data. We are committed to compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and all applicable German data protection laws.

3. Data We Collect

3.1 Personal Information

  • Name, email address, phone number
  • Shipping and billing address
  • Date of birth and gender
  • Payment information (processed securely through third-party providers)

3.2 Health Data

  • Blood biomarker test results
  • DNA analysis results (saliva samples)
  • Health goals and lifestyle information
  • Supplement usage and tracking data from our mobile app

3.3 Technical Data

  • IP address, browser type, device information
  • Usage data and analytics from website and app interactions
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

4. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal and health data for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: Processing and analyzing your test samples, generating personalized health insights, and formulating customized supplements
  • Communication: Sending test results, supplement recommendations, and important service updates
  • App Functionality: Enabling access to the Lab1 mobile app for tracking your health journey and viewing insights
  • Improvement: Analyzing aggregated, anonymized data to improve our services and develop new features
  • Compliance: Meeting legal and regulatory requirements

5. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data based on:

  • Consent: You explicitly consent to the collection and processing of your health data
  • Contract Performance: Processing is necessary to deliver the services you've purchased
  • Legitimate Interests: Improving our services and ensuring security
  • Legal Obligations: Compliance with applicable laws and regulations

6. Data Security

We implement industry-leading security measures to protect your data:

  • Bank-level AES-256 encryption for data at rest and in transit
  • Secure, EU-certified laboratory facilities with strict access controls
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Staff training on data protection and confidentiality
  • Pseudonymization of biological samples (separated from personal identifiers)

7. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell your personal or health data. We only share data with:

  • Laboratory Partners: EU-certified labs that analyze your samples under strict confidentiality agreements
  • Cloud Service Providers: Secure hosting services that meet GDPR requirements
  • Payment Processors: To process transactions securely

All third parties are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for specified purposes.

8. Your Rights Under GDPR

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
  • Right to Rectification: Correct any inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to Object: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time (without affecting prior processing)

To exercise these rights, contact us at max@eterno.health.

9. Data Retention

We retain your data for the following periods:

  • Account Data: Until you request deletion or close your account
  • Health Data: Up to 10 years for medical records compliance, unless you request earlier deletion
  • Biological Samples: Destroyed within 90 days after analysis, unless you consent to retention for research
  • Transaction Records: As required by tax and accounting laws (typically 10 years)

10. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings. Essential cookies are necessary for service functionality.

11. International Data Transfers

Your data is primarily processed within the European Union. If data is transferred outside the EU, we ensure adequate safeguards are in place through Standard Contractual Clauses or other approved mechanisms.

12. Children's Privacy

Lab1 services are intended for individuals 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect data from minors without parental consent.

13. Mobile App Privacy

The Lab1 mobile app collects usage data to provide personalized tracking of your health journey, supplement intake, and progress insights. App data is encrypted and synchronized securely with your account. You can control app permissions through your device settings.

14. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated via email. The "Last updated" date at the top indicates the most recent revision.
